**Data model**

Entity: Feedback

Attributes:

- id (UUID, required, PK)
- productId (UUID, required, indexed)
- customerId (UUID, required, indexed)
- rating (int, required, 1..5)
- comment (varchar, required, 1..2000)
- sentiment (enum: positive/neutral/negative, required, computed)
- createdAt (timestamp, required, UTC)
- updatedAt (timestamp, nullable)

Техническое требование:
Уникальный индекс (customerId, productId).
